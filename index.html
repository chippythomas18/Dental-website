Treatments

We provide a comprehensive range of dental services

General Dentistry

A significant part of comprehensive dental care is ongoing maintenance to minimize the chances of problems occurring, whether you need routine dental care or one of our other high-quality services, we’re here to help.

Orthodontics

it involves the diagnosis, prevention and correction of misaligned teeth and jaws. Apart from aligning teeth, orthodontic treatment helps to restore a person’s bite, the function of their teeth and aesthetics. 

Periodontics

It is the dental specialty focusing exclusively in the inflammatory disease that destroys the gums and other supporting structures around the teeth. Dr. Rosanna is here to deal with these diseases.

Preventive Dentistry

Teeth cleaning and preventive dental care is one of the best possible means of maintaining optimum oral health. Preventive dental care can help each member of your family maintain a beautiful, healthy smile by safeguarding against oral diseases like dental decay, gum disease, or periodontal disease.

Restorative dentistry

It is the study, diagnosis and integrated management of diseases of the teeth and their supporting structures and the rehabilitation of the dentition to functional and aesthetic requirements of the individual. It involves the replacement of missing or damaged teeth. Fillings, crowns (“caps”), bridges and implants.

Extraction

It is the process of removing a tooth or tooth parts which is performed for various reasons including tooth decay that has destroyed enough tooth structure to prevent restoration. Extractions of impacted teeth are also performed as are extractions of some permanent teeth to make space for orthodontic treatment.
